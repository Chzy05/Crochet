Crochet

                                                                                                                                


                                                      Crochet is a type of needlecraft which involves the use of a crochet hook to create

                                          fabric from loops of yarn or threads. It is similar to knitting. But instead of using 2 needles,

                                          crochet uses a single hook. With crochet, you can create a wide variety of items such as

                                          blankets, hats, and even intricate lacework. It's a hobby enjoyed by many people around the

                                          world which offers a fun, creative way to spend your free time.



                                                      Crocheting is a hobby that can help us to be creative, relaxed and happy. Crochet

                                          can be difficult especially if we don't have a guide and no proper knowledge about its process

                                          and steps. That's why we the Group 2 of the 12-HUMSS 12 created a website which can help

                                          you gain the knowledge and help in your crocheting journey which includes the following:


                                          • History and Origin of Crochet


                                          • Materials and Tools of Crochet


                                          • Process of Learning Crochet


                                          • Benefits of Crocheting
